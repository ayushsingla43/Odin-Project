Lemonade

Description:A cool and refreshing drink for a hot and sunny day, lemonade is one of the most popular and easy to make beverages for Summers.This aptly named recipe is as good as it gets: Sweet, tart, easy to throw together, and oh-so refreshing. Sweeten your day with our top-rated lemonade recipe.

Ingredients Required

  • Freshly Squeezed Lemon Juice
  • Water
  • Ice
  • Sugar
  • Mint Leaves to Garnish

Recipe

  1. Mix the lemon juice and water in appropriate proportion in a jug.
  2. Add sugar as per taste.
  3. Add Ice to the mixture to cool it.
  4. Pour the mixture into a glass. Garnish it with mint leaves.
  5. Serve Cool.
