Tea

Description: Tea is one of the most renowned and liked beverages all around the world- known to reduce drowsiness and provide fresh energy in the morning. Tea comes in a variety of flavors, here we shall describe the receipe for a simple cup of chai.

Ingredients Required

  • Milk
  • Tea Powder
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Grinded Masala as per taste

Recipe

  1. Add half a cup of water in a pan. Add a tbsp of Sugar,Grinded Masala and Tea Powder to it each.
  2. Heat the pan on flame until the water starts boiling.
  3. Add 3/4th cup of milk to the pan.
  4. Keep the pan on flame until the mixture starts rising.
  5. Serve hot.
